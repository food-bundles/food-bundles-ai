FoodBundles is a food ordering and trading platform that allows customers to order food bundles and traders to manage their accounts and inventory.

## How To Order
1. Create an account on the FoodBundles platform.
2. Verify your identity using the Authenticator app or OTP.
3. Browse available food bundles and select items.
4. Place your order and choose a payment method.
5. You can also apply a voucher code at checkout to get a discount.

## Order Now Pay Later (BNPL)
FoodBundles offers an Order Now Pay Later feature, allowing customers to receive their food bundles immediately and pay at a later date. This is managed through the Loan and Wallet Management system.

## Trader Account
Traders can register for a Trader Account to list and sell food bundles on the platform. The Trader Dashboard provides an overview of orders, inventory, pricing trends, and earnings.

## Trader Agreement
Traders must agree to the FoodBundles Trader Agreement before listing products. The agreement outlines the terms of service, pricing policies, and responsibilities of traders on the platform.

## Authentication & Security
FoodBundles uses two-factor authentication (2FA) via an Authenticator app or OTP (One-Time Password) to secure user accounts. Users must register and verify with the Authenticator during account setup.

## Market Pricing Trends
The platform provides market pricing trend data to help traders set competitive prices for their food bundles.

## Wallet & Loan Management
Customers and traders have access to a digital wallet for managing payments, loans, and transactions within the FoodBundles ecosystem.
